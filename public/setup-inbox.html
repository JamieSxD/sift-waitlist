Quick Gmail Setup

Set up automatic forwarding with one click

Gmail Setup Instructions

Open Gmail and go to Settings (gear icon) ‚Üí "See all settings"
Click on "Filters and Blocked Addresses" tab
Click "Create a new filter"
In "Has the words" field, enter: unsubscribe
Click "Create filter" ‚Üí Check "Forward it to" and enter your Siftly inbox address
Also check "Keep Gmail's copy in the Inbox" and click "Create filter"
If prompted to add the forwarding address, verify it from your Siftly inbox

Outlook Setup Instructions

Open Outlook.com and go to Settings (gear icon) ‚Üí "View all Outlook settings"
Go to "Mail" ‚Üí "Rules"
Click "Add new rule" ‚Üí Name it "Newsletter Forwarding"
Set condition: "It includes these words" ‚Üí enter unsubscribe
Set action: "Forward the message to" ‚Üí enter your Siftly inbox address
Save the rule - newsletters will now forward automatically to Siftly

Apple Mail Setup Instructions

Open Mail app and go to Mail ‚Üí Rules... (or Preferences ‚Üí Rules)
Click "Add Rule" and name it "Newsletter Forwarding"
Set condition: "Message Content" ‚Üí "Contains" ‚Üí unsubscribe
Set action: "Forward Message" and enter your Siftly inbox address
Save the rule - newsletters containing "unsubscribe" will forward to Siftly

General Email Forwarding

Look for "Rules", "Filters", or "Forwarding" in your email settings
Create a rule/filter with condition: "Message contains" ‚Üí unsubscribe
Set action to forward these emails to your Siftly inbox address
Make sure to keep a copy in your original inbox
